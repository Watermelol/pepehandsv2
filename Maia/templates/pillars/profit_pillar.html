Profitability

  • Info

Profitability is a metric used to determine the scope of a company's profit in relation to the size of the business.
It can also be defined as a business's ability to produce a return on an investment based on its resources in comparison with an alternative investment.
Companies can realize a profit, but not be profitable.
Financial values such as the companies ROA, sales growth, profit margin and ROE are of the utmost importance when measuring Profitability.
